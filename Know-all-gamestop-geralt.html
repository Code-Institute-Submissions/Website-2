About The Witcher

    Geralt of Rivia (Polish: Geralt z Rivii) is a fictional character.
    The protagonist of The Witcher series of short stories and novels by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski.
    He is a magically enhanced monster-hunter known as a "witcher", who also possesses supernatural abilities due to his mutations.
    Geralt of Rivia is 100 Years old.
